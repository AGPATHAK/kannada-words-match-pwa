# Kannada Words Match PWA — 50-Word Candidate Deck Template

## Purpose

Provide the review template that must be filled before deck integration begins.

## Required columns

| ID | Kannada | Devanagari | English gloss | Category | Difficulty band | Intro stage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---:|---|
| mane | ಮನೆ | मने | house | home | core | 1 | clear and concrete |

## Review questions for each row

- Is the English gloss concise and beginner-safe?
- Is the Kannada form canonical?
- Is the Devanagari scaffold acceptable as a support layer?
- Is the item too confusable with another planned item?
- Does this item belong in the first 50, or later?
